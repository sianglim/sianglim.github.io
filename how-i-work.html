Step 1: We talk

I take the time to learn more about your business – what you do, your clients and customers and your goals for the website. I am upfront about what services I can offer. If you are happy to go ahead, we agree on a simple contract and an initial payment to begin work.

Step 2: Content and design

Content is the heart of your website. That means well-written copy and great images are a must. I can offer advice on how to write and present content. I can also write your content as an additional service. Once your content is prepared, we work on the design.

Step 3: Keeping in touch

Creating a great website requires collaboration. I send you updates on the progress of your website and expect you to do the same with timely feedback or requests. Once things are finalised, we sort out the final payment and launch your website.

Step 4: Post-launch

There are always things that need improving. A block of time is set aside for minor updates to your website post-launch. I also help you form a simple website maintenance strategy. Take control of your website by learning simple code to update it or get in touch if you need more help.
